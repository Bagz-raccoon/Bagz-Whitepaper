6) Monthly Burns & 30% Burn Funding

Burns happen monthly. The goal is to reduce supply over time using ecosystem-driven funding mechanics. $BAGZ uses a revenue-linked approach so supply reduction can grow with the ecosystem.

  • 30% of funds brought into the ecosystem (including merchandise, music-related initiatives, NFTs, and other ecosystem activity) are allocated toward buybacks and token burns.
  • Cadence: Burns are executed once per month (not weekly).
  • Transparency: Burn proof is shared through the app hub and can be verified on-chain.
